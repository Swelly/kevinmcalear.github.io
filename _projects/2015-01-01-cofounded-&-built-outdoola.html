Outdoola is an online marketplace for hunting, fishing, and other outdoor adventures. I designed and built the web application and co-founded the company with two other people while all of us were in three different cities. (Brooklyn, NY, Atlanta, GA, and Space Coast, FL) We learned a lot about taking an idea and building it into a product on the side. We also applied to Y Combinator and got rejected but learned a lot from the experience.

Technology Stack: Firebase, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Angular, CSS, HTML, and a few APIs.
