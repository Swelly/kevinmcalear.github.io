Using publicly-available attendance and school performance data from the NYC Department of Education, I created a tool to explore the correlations among zip codes, attendance, and graduation rates in NYC. It was also used in Kinvolved's presentation at NY Tech Meetup (around 1:14:30) and I also gave a talk at Civic Design Camp using this tool.
