Kinvolved is an app to improve PreK-12 student attendance by sharing real-time information among families, schools, and after-school programs. I was the third employee and responsible for all tech aspects of the company as well as actually building the application. You may have seen Kinvolved in things such as Forbes 30 Under 30, FOX News, NPR's Marketplace, Business Insider, NY1, DNAInfo, and many more.

Technology Stack: Ruby, Rails, PostgreSQL, JavaScript, D3, Angular, Bootstrap, CSS, HTML, and a few APIs.
