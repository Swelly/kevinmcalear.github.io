Kinvolved is an app to improve PreK-12 student attendance by sharing real-time information among families, schools, and after-school programs. I was the third employee and responsible for all tech aspects of the company.

Technology Stack: Ruby, Rails, PostgreSQL, JavaScript, D3, Angular, Bootstrap, CSS, HTML, and a few APIs.
